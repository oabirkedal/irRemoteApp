About

irRemote

Purpose

irRemote replaces a TV remote.

This project includes a Bluetooth Low Energy peripheral (nRF52) and the irRemote app.

This is a proof-of-concept rather than something to bring to market. It was created as a coding example for LaunchCode.

Description

The peripheral (nRF52) learns IR codes from the user's TV remote. The app controls the peripheral, including when to learn and send IR signals.

How to Use

Connect to remote:

Go to irRemote page (Home Page). Click 'Find remote.' A display with nearby BLE devices pops up. Select the right peripheral.

Teach IR:

In home page, click the bottom text 'Edit buttons.' Select 'Power.' Aim your TV remote at the peripheral and press the power button on your remote. The power button in the app turns red. In the app, press 'Finish.'

Send IR:

After the above steps, aim peripheral at your TV. In the app, click the power button. The TV should now turn ON/OFF. Repeat the above procedure for volume buttons.
